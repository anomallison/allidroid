``I have the following commands:

!help - Brings up this command

!monster - Generates a basic monster

!friend - Generates a friendly monster

!boss - Generates a boss

!sexymonster - Generates a potentially sexier monster

!roll - Rolls a dice, or a string. XdY format, with X being the number of dice and Y being the sides per dice. Alternatively Y can be a string of characters

!gay - Puts a coin in the gay jar

!lesbian - Generates a lesbian

!lesbifriend - Generates a friend lesbian

!slash - Generates a slashfic prompt, defaults to any two characters from any list; a character have a specified gender, gender and character list, or gender, character list and sublist; the base pool can also be modified the same way
eg: !slash !slash f/f !slash f/f da !slash f;me/f;fe3h;blackeagles/f;ow !slash f/f/f g9

!generatename - Generates a name, optionally can take a maximum phoneme count, or a maximum and a minimum phoneme count, however maximum cannot be less than minimum, and both must be above 0 and less than 100

!generateartifact - Generates a magical artifact...

!how - type in a thing like "how delicious is gravy" or "how good are cats" and it will respond, echoing your sentiments

!gacha - Take your chances and receive... heroes? with rarity! It means just as much as any other gacha game! You can also give it a number, and it'll pull that many, up to 12

!princess - Generates a weird princess. It is a verbose command.

!remindme - This sets a reminder in allidroid to be sent to the channel which the command is used. It has a delay (formated like 1d2h3m4s), and then the message which Allidroid will say
eg: !remindme 2h take your meds! !remindme in 4h30m do your assignment

!removereminder - This removes a reminder from Allidroid, by taking a reminderid (which is given when a reminder is set)

!feedback - gives a link to the Google Form to give AnomAllison, my creator, feedback about Allidroid
