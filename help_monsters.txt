``!monster`` generates a random monster with brief descriptors

``!friend`` generates a random friend with brief descriptors

``!sexymonster`` generates a random sexymonster with brief descriptors

``!boss`` generates a more elaborate boss monster, who is named and may have equipment and more descriptive usually

``!monster``, ``!friend``, ``!sexymonster`` take no arguments, simply type and go

``!boss`` takes extra keywords as arguments, if you want higher chance of certain types of descriptions.

the following are keywords:
arcane, alchemy, holy, martial, karate, roguish, cyberpunk, necromantic, gothic, psionic, clothes, anthromorphic, loadout, item, wings, tail, friend, himbo, twink, ally, firearm
