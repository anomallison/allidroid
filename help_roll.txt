``!roll`` is a dice rolling command, using standard dice notation

``!roll`` takes 1 argument in the form of *x*d*y*d*z*+*c* or *x*d*y*d*z*-*c*, where *x* is the number of dice to roll, *y* is the sides per die, *z* is the number of lowest die to drop, and *c* is added to the total.
It can also be shortened to *x*d*y*+*c* or *x*d*y*-*c* or even just *x*d*y*

examples: ``!roll 4d6d1``
``!roll 2d20d1+7``
``!roll 4d6+4``
``!roll 1d20-3``
