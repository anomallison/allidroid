``!generatemap`` generates a low resolution hex grid terrain map

``!generatemap`` takes upto 6 arguments currently: the width, the height, grid opacity, the number of landmasses to generate on the map, the 'hot/cold' cold balance between 0 and 1, and the climate variance between hexes between 0 and 1

example
``!generatemap`` 40 30 0 8 0.51 0.22
``!generatemap`` 150 100 0.33 15
``!generatemap`` 32 18 0.5
``!generatemap``
