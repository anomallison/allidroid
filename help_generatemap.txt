``!generatemap`` generates a low resolution hex grid terrain map

``!generatemap`` takes upto 5 arguments currently: the width, the height, grid opacity, the number of landmasses to generate on the map, and the 'hot/cold' cold balance between 0 and upto about 400 (default is 125)

example
``!generatemap 40 30 0 8 166``
``!generatemap 150 100 0.33 15``
``!generatemap 32 18 0.5``
``!generatemap``
